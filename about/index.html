About

Hi, I’m Mark Mossberg.

tl;dr: I like making and breaking things.

I’m a junior at Northeastern University, studying Computer Engineering. My main interests include operating and embedded systems, and systems exploitation and reverse engineering. You might also catch me trying my hand at web/app development, but don’t tell anyone ;).

On campus I’m involved with IEEE, Wireless Club, the Northeastern CCDC team, and NU Hacks. Sometimes I play CTF, and in February 2015 I played in SECCON with Shellphish.

In my free time I enjoy slacklining, playing guitar, and working on various projects. In April 2014, I placed in the top three for a Massachusetts cybersecurity competition.
